About CPLS

Capacity Building Professional Development & Leadership Society

CPLS is the premier student society of the Department of Pharmacy, dedicated to fostering professional excellence, leadership skills, and academic growth among pharmacy students.

Through workshops, seminars, community outreach, and industry engagement, CPLS prepares tomorrow's pharmacists for a dynamic healthcare landscape.
